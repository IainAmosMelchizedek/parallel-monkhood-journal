Parallel Monkhood Journal

A simple front door into your Git‑based monastic practice space.

Welcome to your experimental monkhood console. Use the tabs below to generate Markdown for your GitHub repository: modules, bhakti‑yoga, meditation, and festivals. Copy the output into the corresponding files under modules/, bhakti-yoga/, meditation/, or festivals/.
